Daily Habit Tracker

Enter health habits that you want to track for the day. Once a habit is entered it is saved to local storage. There is a checkbox to indicate that the habit was completed. Changing state of the checkbox will trigger a new save for that habit. Below are some examples of habits to track. Note: duplicate tasks are overwritten in the database, so be sure the wording of each task is unique.

  • drink water 8 oz at 8am
  • morning stretch
  • 30 min cardio exercise
  • water 8 oz at 9:30 am
  • walk for 1 mile approx 2000 steps
  • eat a healthy lunch
  • more cardio: rapid walk to jog 2 miles 4000 steps
  • weight bearing exercise
  • strength training
  • eat a healthy dinner
  • 5-10 min after dinner walk
