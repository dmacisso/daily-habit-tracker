Daily Habit Tracker

Select or enter health habits that you want to track, and check them off as you complete them. Once a habit is entered, it is saved to local storage with the current date-time stamp. Changing state of the checkbox will trigger a new save for that habit and will update the date-time stamp. Buttons: The "Clear Habit" button, will clear the entry from the screen but remains in the database. The "Delete Habit" button will delete the entry from the database. The other buttons are self explanatory.
