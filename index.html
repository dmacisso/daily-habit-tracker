Daily Habit Tracker

Enter health habits that you want to track for the day, and check them off as you complete them. Once a habit is entered, it is saved to local storage with the current date-time stamp. Changing state of the checkbox will trigger a new save for that habit and will update the date-time stamp. Buttons: The "Clear Habit" button, will clear the entry from the screen but remains in the database. The "Delete Habit" button will delete the entry from the database. The other buttons are self explanatory.

Some examples of habits to track are listed below.

  • drink water 8 oz
  • morning stretch
  • 30 min cardio
